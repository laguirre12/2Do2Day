About

Touday(too-day) is a webpage that gives users advice on what to do for a particular event. The webpage also gives the user the freedom to add advice to the website.

For example, if the user is at work. They can click work, and an advice would pop up on the screen. The user can either take that advice or click the refresh button that would show a different advice. This webpage is used to get people out of their comfort zone and motivate them to try new things.
